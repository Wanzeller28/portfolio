Zoofy

Role: Lead UX/UI designer

Tools Used: Figma

Product: Mobile application

Duration: 2 months

My Responsabilities

When I joined the team, my primary goal was to improve the usability of the app and enhance user engagement. The team was seeking innovative ways to motivate users to interact with Zoofy more frequently and meaningfully.

To achieve this, I rethought the app's strategy, focusing on two key aspects:

  • Creating a Circular Economy: I designed features that promote sustainable interactions among users, encouraging repeat usage and value exchange within the platform.
  • Incorporating Gamification: By integrating game-like elements, I sought to make the user experience more engaging and rewarding, fostering motivation and long-term loyalty.
