DecideAct

Company: DecideAct

Role: UX/UI design

Tools Used: Figma

Product: Web application

Duration: 2 months

The Objective

To create a modern, visually cohesive website that reflects the company's new branding. The goal was to project a confident and trustworthy image, clearly communicating the company's core values and effectively showcasing its software offerings.

My Role

I focused on redesigning the user interface to align with the updated brand identity, incorporating clean layouts, intuitive navigation, and engaging visuals. The new design emphasizes clarity and professionalism, ensuring users quickly understand the company's mission and the benefits of its software products.
