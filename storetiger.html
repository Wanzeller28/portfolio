The Challenge

A platform that facilitates the rental of self-storage spaces, such as garages, lockers, and commercial units, connecting clients with property owners in a user-friendly and secure digital experience.

The Solution

We developed an intuitive interface featuring a simplified search system with effective filters and an optimized booking flow to enhance the user experience. The platform also includes a detailed user profile area where clients can track their bookings and manage payments easily.

My Role

As the lead Product Designer, I collaborated with a cross-functional team that included the main stakeholders and the developers.

Responsibilities

  • Analyzed similar platforms to gather best practice references.
  • Designed user flows and created high-fidelity mockups and prototypes in Figma, ensuring alignment with business requirements.
  • Developed a design system in Figma to maintain consistency across the project.
  • Worked closely with the development team to ensure the accurate implementation of designs.
